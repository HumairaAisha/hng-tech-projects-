About Me

Biography

Hello! I'm Aisha Muhyiddeen Ahmad, a passionate frontend developer and creative problem-solver from Nigeria. I love building clean, responsive, and user-friendly web experiences that combine thoughtful design with strong functionality.
Beyond code, I believe in community and innovation.

Goals in this program

My main goal in this program is to grow into a confident, independent developer who can build functional and user-friendly web applications from scratch. I want to strengthen my understanding of frontend, improve my ability to structure projects, and learn to think critically about design and user experience. Most importantly, I want to finish the program with a sense of clarity, knowing how far I’ve come and how much more I can build.

Areas of low confidence

I sometimes struggle with self-doubt, especially when I don’t understand a concept as fast as I expect to. Debugging and advanced JavaScript logic can still feel overwhelming at times. But I’ve learned to slow down, ask better questions, and give myself permission to grow at my own pace. Every challenge now feels like an opportunity to become a little more patient and resilient.
